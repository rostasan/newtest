Marketing

Be a marketing partner

Marketing partners are groups or organizations that help us get the word out to people who game and we want to show our appreciation by giving back. To qualify a gaming group or organization wishing to become a partner will need preregister and give a group affiliation. Marketing partners who let us know ahead of time, and we list their name as a possible affiliate when people register online or on-site. For every person who selects your group as an affiliate, we will set aside $1 for every paid one day registration, and $2 for every paid two day registration. At the end of the convention, we will send you the total amount set aside for your group.

How can I partner with Wolfcon?

There are lots of ways - and they aren't mutually exclusive - we would love for you to participate in as many of these as you can.

  • Reserve a table at our Bizarre Bazaar (Sat 10am - 8pm)
  • Be a Vendor
  • Exchange links, banners and/or flyers
  • Be a marketing partner
  • Be a Special guest
  • Be an event partner
  • Be a Sponsor
  • Volunteer
  • Attend
