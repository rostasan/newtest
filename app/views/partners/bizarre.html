Bizarre

Reserve a table at our Bizarre Bazaar (Sat 10am - 8pm) This summer we hope to create a grand marketplace for people who love games. The rules are simple. The Bazaar runs from 10am to 8pm on Sat, and anyone can reserve a table for $10. We encourage stores to bring product they are ready to take off their shelves - or their best selling products - whatever they think will move the best. Whoever reserves a table can only have as much product as will fit on their table - but they can replenish their stock if they desire. They may hang a banner from their table, or decorate their table, but not in a way that takes up more space. Anyone with a paid admission may reserve a table for the Bazaar for $5.
