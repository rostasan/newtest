Are you game?

If ten years of running Wolfcon has taught us anything, it is that we cannot do it alone. We need the support of gaming groups. We need the support of mfr's, distributors, gamers, vendors, and other interested parties. We can supply experience, technology and connections. We can be the nexus that brings all these people and things together, but we cannot do it all on our own. We need partners. So...are you game?
